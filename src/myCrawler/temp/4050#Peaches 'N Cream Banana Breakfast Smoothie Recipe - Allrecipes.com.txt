-------------
Peaches 'N Cream Banana Breakfast Smoothie Recipe - Allrecipes.com
-------------
1 1/4 cups milk 
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt 
1 banana, broken into chunks 
1 packet peaches and cream flavor instant oatmeal 
2 packets granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda&#174;) (optional) 
5 ice cubes 

-------------
Place the milk, yogurt, banana, instant oatmeal, sweetener, and ice cubes into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
